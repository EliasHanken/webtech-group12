The grand story of KRR; our Values and inspiration

The KRR started out with a simple idea in the city of Ålesund: Bring easy access to the usage of bikes and give people the option to pick the healthier choice. Being healthy and saving the environment should be easy and fun! The first KRR shop was set up in 2015 around Brosundet by Wenke Heel after a small loan of only 1 million dollars from her father. The shop gathered customers quickly and an expansion to Moa was quickly made.

Today KRR has a huge collection of 120 bikes available from one of two pickup points which are continually being upgraded every month according to the latest trends! KRR has also been featured in huge newspaper around the world like New York times, CNN and finnish MTV3! KRR is also planning on opening new workshops in London, Berlin, Tokyo and even Cairo! So don't miss out on what is easily becoming a global phenomena! Sign up and rent our bikes in the most easy and simple way possible, and perhaps you too will find out exactly what makes KRR so special and Keep Rollin' rolling' rollin' till your feet explode from exhaustion!
